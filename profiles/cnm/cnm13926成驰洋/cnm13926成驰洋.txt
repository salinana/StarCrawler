简介:成驰洋，男，籍贯：湖北省宜昌市。美术专业出身的音乐制作人，在音乐律动中寻找灵感的平面设计师。2007年至2009年与好友吴成蛟组成“TBCREW”发行两张组合专辑《Deeplayer》与《Ladies Around The World》。2010年带来全新的爵士说唱实体专辑《自然的赋予》。2011年加入Urban Magic Records之后增添电子曲风继续创作HipHop音乐，代表作品《2011》、《Excitant》等等。2012年与天津音乐人胡家璇合作发行实体专辑《起源》。2013年与好友镜子（微胖女神）、插画师炒饭成立顽色音乐品牌创意机构，专注于为年轻艺人们打造个性概念的音乐作品周边以及各大音乐相关活动的整体策划与包装。From 2007 to 2009, he has released two formal group albums, 《Deeplayer》, and《Ladies Around The World》, as a member of ‘TBCREW’ with Chengjiao Wu. Later in 2010, he came back with new Jazz rap album 《自然的赋予》(‘gift from the great nature’ as translated). In 2011, he officially joined Urban Magic Records and continued on his way of hip-hop music production. Since then, he has been trying to produce electric hip-hop and brought out works as representative as 《2011》 and 《Excitant》. In the past 2012, he released the very new Album《起源》(‘the Origin’ as translated) in collaboration with a Tian Jin musician, Jiaxuan Hu.
